Hip & Trendy places

Looking for a cute hipster place to eat at, that is picture worthy? If so, Houston is the perfect place to take multiple foodagram worthy pictures. If you are looking for a combination of both lunch-dinner, then you are in luck because Houston goes hard at delivering the best brunch dishes. Some popular places include, The Breakfast Klub, Snooze an A.M Eatery, and Dish society, each one of these establishments offers more than one location in the metropolitan area, talk about accessibility and convenience.

Photo by Robin Asbell via Coop

Fine dinning

Home to multiple award-winning restaurants, Houston has one of the best culinary spots in the country. Some delicious must stop places to eat at are Saltgrass, Hugo’s, Pappasito’s Cantina, and Churrascos. Saltgrass is an American steakhouse, offering one of the best BBQ in the South. Both Pappasito’s and Hugo’s offer primarily mexican food, with the exception of Pappasito’s wçhich combines texan food, from here the word tex-mex surges. Churrascos offers a variety of exotic Latin American food that goes beyond the Northern Continent, it has central and south american fine-cuisine.
